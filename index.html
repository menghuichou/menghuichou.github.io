Google Crowdsource

Posted on 2022-01-14 Edited on 2022-01-15 In uncategories Symbols count in article: 1.2k Reading time ≈ 1 mins.

In this past summer, I was selected as one of 48 participants among 500+ applicants to participate in the first Google Crowdsource virctual camp in Taiwan. I got a chance to engange in learning data science, machine learning, and basic concepts in AI with Google team in 2021 summer. At the end of the camp, my teammates and I did a challening project which foucused on how to promote Google Crowdsource and improve its user experience.

Based on historical statics provided by Google Crowdsource, our team decided to emphasize on user interface, making the product be more user-oriented and user-friendly by adding graphical instructions, front page, and icons. We also suggested the Google Crowdsource developing team to make the product be more attractive by adding pay-per-quality rewarding system and competitions. These features may attract more potential customers to stay around on website and try the product.

My team was able to make it to the end and presented our idea at front in pitching time. We received many useful and positive feedbacks towards our presentation and ideas from other teams and mentors. Although the camp was held online this year, it not only helped me become a better thinker and communicater but also gave me a chance to think into a real world problem and solve it.
